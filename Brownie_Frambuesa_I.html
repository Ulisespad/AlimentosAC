Raspberry brownies

Ingredients:

1/2 cup of butter

½ cup of white sugar

¾ cup of brown sugar

¾ cup of cocoa powder

2 eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 teaspoon baking powder

¾ cup of flour

Sweet Masseube of Raspberry

¾ cup of caramel

Preparation:

Preheat the oven to 180 ° C.

Lightly greasing a plate with butter paper

In a bowl, microwave the mateca, cocoa powder and all the sugar for 40 seconds.

Add the eggs and the vanilla extract to the mixture. Shake. Then add the baking powder and the flour.

Add Sweet Masseube from Raspberry to taste.

Pour half of the mixture on the plate evenly. Then spread the caramel. Complete above with the rest of the mixture.

Take to oven for 25/30 minutes
