Banana jam

Ingredients:

3 bananas (320 grams approx.)

190 grams of sugar

150 grams of water

a few drops of vanilla extract

1/2 lemon

Preparation:

Peel the bananas and cut them into thin slices, put them in a bowl and sprinkle them with the juice of half a lemon.

Put a saucepan on the fire with water and sugar, heat to medium high temperature until the sugar dissolves and the syrup begins to take a little color.

Add the banana and the vanilla extract to the saucepan, raise the heat and when it begins to bubble, reduce it.

Cook at medium-low heat, stirring occasionally and breaking the fruit with the wooden spoon, for about 20 minutes or until it has the texture of a jam. Remember that cooling will thicken more.

Store the banana jam in a jar with a tight lid and reserve in the fridge, ready for tasting.
