Strawberry frape

Ingredients:

1 1/2 Frozen Strawberry Mug

1 LECHERA® Condensed Milk Bottle Serves

Easy Taste with Lemon Pay

4 fresh mint leaves, disinfected to decorate

1 1/2 Cup Ice frappé

1/4 Cup Sugar

1/3 Water Cup

Preparation:

Serve the previous mixture in a glass halfway, add a little LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk Serves Easy Taste with Lemon Pay and pour a little more of the strawberry mixture. Finish with more LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk Serves Easy Lemon Pay Taste.

Decorate with slices of strawberries and mint leaves; offers.

Remember that to frappear the ice in the blender, you must do it little by little.

Strawberries are low in calories, one cup contains 43 kcal.
