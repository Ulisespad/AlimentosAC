Strawberry milk shake

Ingredients:

One liter of Crem Strawberry ice cream

Strawberries

Small glass of milk

Condensed milk

Crushed ice

Chantilly

Preparation:

the blender, place two tablespoons of Crem Ice cream strawberry, a glass of milk, crushed ice, condensed milk and some strawberries. Blend them until they mix well.

Then, serve the milkshake in a glass with skinny and add on top pieces of strawberry and a little Chantilly
