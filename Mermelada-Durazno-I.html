Peach jam

Ingredients:

5 kg ripe and healthy peaches

2 1/2 kg sugar

2 lemons

Preparation:

1.Wash the peaches and cut them into thick pieces removing the stones.

2.Put the pieces of peaches in a saucepan and add the juice of the lemons and y piece of the rind only the yellow part.

3.Boil slowly stirring and foaming for 10 '

4.Step on the peaches forming a puree.

5.Vorte the mash to the saucepan and add the sugar and stir quickly so that it does not burn until it takes a point.

6.Fill the jars dry and clean immediately, let cool and cover hermetically and keep in the refrigerator placed upside down.
