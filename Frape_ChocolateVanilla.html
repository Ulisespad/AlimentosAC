Chocolate wiht vanilla frape

Ingredients:

1/4 Water Cup

2 vanilla ice cream balls

1 1/2 Tablespoon 100% pure soluble coffee NESCAFÉ® CLÁSICO®

1/4 Cup Milk

2 Tablespoons Cream to beat

Preparation:

Decorate the glass with the chocolate syrup.

Blend NESCAFÉ® CLÁSICO® Soluble Coffee with water, milk and vanilla ice cream.

Serve in a glass and decorate with the whipped cream.

If you wish you can add a little chocolate syrup at the time of liquefying.

Drink coffee because it is a low calorie drink and high in antioxidants.
