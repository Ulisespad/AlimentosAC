Vanilla with peach cake

Ingredients:

1 cup butter or margarine, softened

2 cups of white sugar

4 eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

3 cups of flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 cups fresh peaches, boneless and chopped

Preparation:

1.Preheat the oven to 165 ° C (325 ° F). Grease a screw mold 25 centimeters in diameter and cover it with a thin layer of sugar.

2.Beat the butter and sugar in a large bowl until crisp. While stirring, add the eggs one by one, beating well after each addition, then add the vanilla.

3.Reserve 1/4 cup of flour and hover the rest along with the baking powder and salt. Add these powders to the bowl with the creamy mixture.

4.Cover the peaches with the reserved flour and wrap them in the dough. Pour the dough into the mold, distributing it evenly.

5.Bake until a toothpick comes out clean, between 60 and 70 minutes approximately. Remove from the oven and leave inside the mold for 10 minutes. Unmold and place on a rack to cool completely.
