Blueberrry jam

Nutritional values:

Energy 222

Protein 0.53

Total Fat (g) 0

Cholesterol (mg) 0

Carbohydrates 58.7

Ingredients:

3/4 cup (150 grams) of standard sugar

1 teaspoon (5 milliliters) of lemon juice

Preparation:

1.Place the blackberries in a saucepan and heat over medium heat for 1 minute. Then add the sugar and lemon, and let it boil.

2.Reduce the heat to medium-low and let the jam boil gently and continuously, stirring occasionally, until it thickens a little, between 15 and 20 minutes.

3.Allow to cool and refrigerate to thicken more. Keep refrigerated in a jar with lid (lasts 1 week).
