Banana frape

Ingredients:

2 Cups COFFEE MATE® Liquid

Flavor Cinnamon

1 1/2 Teaspoon 100% pure soluble coffee NESCAFÉ® CLÁSICO®

2 Pieces Banana without shell

6 Ice Cups

Preparation:

Blend the ice until crushed, add the Liquid Cream Substitute for Coffee COFFEE-MATE® Liquid Flavor Cinnamon with the banana and Soluble Coffee NESCAFE® CLÁSICO®; Blends until fully integrated.

Serve in glasses and offers.

Decorate the glass with liquid chocolate.

Banana is a fruit rich in potassium and magnesium, two essential minerals for the contraction of muscles.
