Banana with Strawberry malted

Ingredients:

1 LA LECHERA® Tin in Balance without Fat *

1 Piece Banana

1 CARNATION® CLAVEL® Evaporated Milk Can in Reduced Fat Balance *

7 Pieces Strawberry

Preparation:

Blend CARNATION® CLAVEL® Evaporated Milk 80% Less Fat with LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk, strawberries and banana.

Serves in glasses and offers cold.

It is recommended to frost the glass with cinnamon and sugar, decorate with half a brew and a thick slice of banana.

Strawberry is composed of 85% water, is a fruit with high content of vitamin E and a powerful natural antioxidant that prevents cell aging, also contains vitamin C that helps prevent diseases in the respiratory tract and iron that helps strengthen the immune system.
