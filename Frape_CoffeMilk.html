Coffe with milk frape

Ingredients:

3 Ice Cups

30 milliliters water 2 tablespoons

2 1/2 Tablespoons Sugar

8 Tablespoons Sugar

4 Tablespoons 100% pure soluble coffee NESCAFÉ® CLÁSICO®

1 Tablespoon 100% pure soluble coffee NESCAFÉ® CLÁSICO®

3/4 Milk Cup

Preparation:

For the frappé, liquefy the milk with a spoonful of 100% Pure Soluble NESCAFE® CLASICO® Coffee, two and a half tablespoons of sugar and ice, reserve.

For intense coffee, mix the water with 100% Pure Soluble NESCAFÉ® CLÁSICO® Coffee and the remaining sugar.

Place a little of the intense coffee mixture at the bottom of the glass, a layer of frappé, an intense coffee and repeat the procedure until the glass is full. Offers.

Remember that to frappear the ice, you must add it little by little in the blender.
